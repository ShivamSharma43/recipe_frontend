Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Khoya (Mawa)
  • Oil

Recipe

  1. Make the Dough - Mix khoya, all-purpose flour, baking soda, and a little milk to form a soft, smooth dough.
  2. Shape & Fry - Roll the dough into small balls and deep-fry them on low heat until golden brown.
  3. Prepare Sugar Syrup - Boil sugar, water, cardamom, and rose water to make a light syrup.
  4. Soak & Serve - Soak the fried Gulab Jamun in warm syrup for a few hours, then serve warm or chilled.

Kaju Katli

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Cashews (Kaju)
  • Ghee

Recipe

  1. Grind cashews into a fine powder.
  2. Cook sugar and water to make a one-string consistency syrup.
  3. Mix in the cashew powder and cook until it forms a smooth dough.
  4. Roll, flatten, cut into diamond shapes, and let it set.

Gajar ka Halwa

Ingredients

  • Carrots (Gajar)
  • Milk
  • Sugar

Recipe

  1. Grate carrots and cook them in milk until soft.
  2. Add sugar and stir until it dissolves.
  3. Mix in ghee and cook until the mixture thickens.
  4. Garnish with cardamom, nuts, and serve warm.

Rasmalai

Ingredients

  • Paneer (Chhena)
  • Milk
  • Sugar

Recipe

  1. Boil milk, curdle it with lemon juice, and strain to make paneer.
  2. Knead the paneer, shape into small discs, and cook in sugar syrup.
  3. Reduce milk with saffron, sugar, and cardamom.
  4. Soak the paneer discs in the flavored milk and serve chilled.

Rasgulla

Ingredients

  • Paneer (Chhena)
  • Sugar
  • Water

Recipe

  1. Boil milk, curdle it with lemon juice, and strain to make paneer.
  2. Knead the paneer, shape into smooth balls, and boil in sugar syrup.
  3. Simmer until the rasgullas expand and turn soft.
  4. Cool in the syrup and serve chilled.
